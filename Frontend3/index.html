Rules
Core Rules
1
Every player selects one favourite team at the start of the season. This cannot be changed once locked in.
2
On any match day involving your favourite team, you must vote for your favourite team — no exceptions.
3
On match days that do not involve your favourite team, you are free to vote for either team playing.
4
A correct prediction earns you +1 point.
5
A daily poll will be shared before each match. Failure to vote before the deadline = 0 points for that match. No exceptions.
6
Payment first. No exceptions. 🙏
7
In the event of a tie on total points at season's end, the prize is split equally among all tied players.
8
Rules may be updated if loopholes are discovered. Stay sharp.
